# Booking Timing & Strategy Guide

## When to Book

| Travel Type | Booking Window | Sweet Spot |
|-------------|---------------|------------|
| Domestic US flights | 1-3 months before departure | ~50 days out |
| International flights | 2-8 months before departure | ~3-4 months out |
| Hotels (peak season) | 2-6 months ahead | As early as possible |
| Hotels (off-peak) | Last-minute can work | 1-2 weeks out |
| Rental cars | Book early, re-check weekly | Prices fluctuate; free cancellation is standard |

## Cheapest Days

- **To fly**: Tuesday and Wednesday are typically cheapest departure days
- **To book**: No proven "best day to buy" — but fare sales often launch Tuesday evenings
- **To avoid**: Friday and Sunday departures are most expensive; avoid holiday weekends

## Flexible Date Tips

- Use Google Flights date grid or "Flexible dates" to see ±3 days pricing
- Shoulder season (just before/after peak) offers 30-50% savings
- Consider positioning flights (fly from a cheaper nearby airport)
- Red-eye and early morning flights are typically 10-20% cheaper

## Comparison Workflow (Recommended Order)

1. **Start with Google Flights/Hotels** — get a baseline price and understand the market
2. **Check aggregators** (Skyscanner, KAYAK, Momondo) — find alternative routings and prices
3. **Check deal sites** (Going.com, Secret Flying, Travelzoo) — look for active flash sales
4. **Check credit card portal** — see if points/portal booking beats cash price
5. **Submit rental cars to AutoSlash** — for ongoing price monitoring
6. **Book direct with airline/hotel when possible** — for better change/cancel policies and loyalty credit
