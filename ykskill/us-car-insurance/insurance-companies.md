## Detailed Profiles

### State Farm
- **Telematics**: Drive Safe & Save
- **Discounts**: Multi-policy, multi-car, good driver, good student, defensive driving
- **Online Experience**: Moderate — agent-focused
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Highest among major insurers

### Progressive
- **Telematics**: Snapshot
- **Discounts**: Name Your Price, multi-car, online quote, continuous coverage, homeowner
- **Online Experience**: Excellent
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Average

### GEICO
- **Telematics**: DriveEasy
- **Discounts**: 18 discount types — most in industry, 25% multi-car, military, federal employee
- **Online Experience**: Excellent
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Above average

### Allstate
- **Telematics**: Drivewise
- **Discounts**: Safe driver, multi-policy, diminishing deductible, new car, anti-theft
- **Online Experience**: Good
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Average

### USAA
- **Telematics**: SafePilot
- **Discounts**: Military deployment, storage, family, multi-car, good driver
- **Online Experience**: Excellent
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Highest overall
- **Note**: Eligibility restricted to active military, veterans, and their immediate families

### Liberty Mutual
- **Telematics**: RightTrack
- **Discounts**: Multi-policy, multi-car, early shopper, homeowner
- **Online Experience**: Good
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Below average

### Farmers
- **Telematics**: Signal
- **Discounts**: Multi-line, good driver, good student, anti-theft, transfer discount
- **Online Experience**: Moderate — agent-focused
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Average

### Nationwide
- **Telematics**: SmartRide (up to 40% savings)
- **Discounts**: Multi-policy, multi-car, SmartRide, good student, anti-theft, paperless
- **Online Experience**: Good
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Above average

### Travelers
- **Telematics**: IntelliDrive
- **Discounts**: Multi-policy, multi-car, new car, hybrid/EV, continuous coverage
- **Online Experience**: Moderate
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Above average

### American Family
- **Telematics**: KnowYourDrive
- **Discounts**: Multi-policy, multi-car, loyalty, good student, smart student
- **Online Experience**: Moderate
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Above average

### Erie
- **Telematics**: YourTurn
- **Discounts**: Multi-policy, multi-car, pay-in-full, group, good student
- **Online Experience**: Basic
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Highest J.D. Power score
- **Note**: Available in Northeast/Midwest only (12 states + DC)

### The Hartford
- **Telematics**: TrueLane
- **Discounts**: AARP member, multi-car, multi-policy, loyalty, anti-theft, defensive driving
- **Online Experience**: Good
- **Claims Satisfaction**: Good for 50+ demographic
