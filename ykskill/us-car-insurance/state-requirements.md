# State Minimum Auto Insurance Requirements

Liability format: **Bodily Injury per person / Bodily Injury per accident / Property Damage** (in thousands of dollars).
Example: 25/50/25 = $25,000 per person, $50,000 per accident bodily injury, $25,000 property damage.

## No-Fault States Summary

The following 12 states + DC use a no-fault system: FL, HI, KS, KY (choice), MA, MI, MN, NJ, ND, NY, PA (choice), UT.

In no-fault states, your own PIP coverage pays for your injuries regardless of fault, up to your PIP limit.

## Recommendation

These are minimums only. Most financial advisors recommend at least **100/300/100** liability to adequately protect your assets. If you have significant assets (home, savings), consider an umbrella policy.

National average full coverage cost (2026): **$208/month (~$2,496/year)**.
