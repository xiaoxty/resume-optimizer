# Premium Estimation Reference

## Estimation Procedure

To estimate a user's annual premium:

1. **Start with state average** — look up in the State Average Premiums table below
2. **Apply coverage level factor**:
   - Minimum (state min only): × 0.45
   - Standard (100/300/100): × 0.85
   - Full (comprehensive + collision): × 1.0
3. **Apply age factor** — look up in Age Factor table
4. **Apply driving record factor**:
   - Clean: × 0.85
   - Minor violations: × 1.1
   - Major violations: × 1.5
   - DUI: × 2.0
5. **Apply credit tier factor**:
   - Excellent: × 0.80
   - Good: × 1.00
   - Fair: × 1.30
   - Poor: × 1.80
6. **Apply mileage factor**:
   - < 5,000 miles/year: × 0.85
   - 5,000-10,000: × 0.95
   - 10,000-15,000: × 1.00 (baseline)
   - 15,000-20,000: × 1.10
   - > 20,000: × 1.20
7. **Apply homeowner discount** (if applicable): × 0.92
8. **Present as ±20% range** around the calculated midpoint

## Tips to Lower Premium

- Compare quotes from at least 3-5 insurers
- Consider raising deductible to $1,000 (saves 15-25%)
- Bundle with homeowner's/renters insurance (saves 5-15%)
- Ask about telematics/usage-based programs
- Check all applicable discounts (see discounts-reference.md)
- Improving credit score can significantly reduce rates
- Under 25? Complete defensive driving course for 5-10% discount
