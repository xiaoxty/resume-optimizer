# Seedance 2.0 视频生成 — 提示词编写规范

## 提示词公式
主体描述 + 动作/运动 + 镜头运动 + 场景/环境 + 视觉风格 + 约束/负面提示

## 最佳长度
- 英文: 30-100 词
- 中文: 50-150 字

## 镜头运动词汇
orbit, dolly-in, dolly-out, pan left/right, tracking shot, handheld, aerial, crane shot, static, zoom in, zoom out, push in, pull out, tilt up/down, dutch angle, whip pan, rack focus

## 素材引用
使用 @素材名 引用已上传的素材

## 质量提升技巧
- 慢动作: "slow motion", "120fps slow-mo"
- 特定光照: golden hour, rim lighting, volumetric fog, neon glow, backlit, chiaroscuro, soft diffused light
- 画面质感: cinematic, film grain, anamorphic lens flare, shallow depth of field, bokeh
- 色彩风格: desaturated, warm tones, cool blue palette, high contrast

## 负面提示（放在提示词末尾）
no distortion, no artifacts, no morphing, stable composition, no jitter, no flickering, smooth motion

## 多模态输入
支持最多 9 张图片 + 3 个视频 + 3 个音频作为参考输入

## 示例
"A golden retriever running through a sunlit meadow, ears flapping in slow motion. Tracking shot following the dog from a low angle. Wildflowers and tall grass in the foreground, rolling hills in the background. Golden hour lighting with warm lens flare. Cinematic, shallow depth of field. No distortion, smooth motion."
